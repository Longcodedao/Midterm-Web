SMARTAGRI

SmartAgri is a technology company in the field of
environmental protection and optimization of
natural resources, founded in March 2023 by
a group of Computer Science students in Vietnam.

SmartAgri's technologies include:

  • Using renewable energy
  • Treating wastewater
  • Reducing production waste
  • Using recycled materials
  • Developing smart farming technology
  • Monitoring and Managing the environment

SmartAgri Company has a mission and commitment to protect the environment and promote sustainable development. We provide products and services to help businesses and organizations reduce their negative impact on the environment and increase resource efficiency.
